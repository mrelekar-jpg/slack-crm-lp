Frequently Asked Questions

What is Slack CRM?

Slack CRM is the fastest way to get started with CRM on the Salesforce platform. It lives inside Slack, where your team already works, and uses Slackbot to handle the heavy lifting — so you're up and running in minutes, not weeks.

What can I do with Slack CRM?

Track deals on a visual pipeline, manage contacts and accounts, capture leads from web forms, handle support cases, and let Slackbot help with meeting prep, account research, follow-up emails, and record updates — all inside Slack.

How much does it cost?

Slack CRM is included in the Slack Business+ plan at no additional cost. Start with a free trial — no credit card required. Supports up to 100 users.

Do I need to set up a Salesforce account separately?

No. When you opt in to Slack CRM, a Salesforce workspace is provisioned automatically in the background. You manage everything from Slack — no separate login needed.

What happens when my team outgrows Slack CRM?

Upgrade whenever you're ready. All your records and history are already in Salesforce, so there's nothing to move. You just get access to more.

Can my whole team use it?

Yes. Slack CRM supports up to 100 users on your team. Everyone sees the same customer records, deal updates, and conversation context — no per-seat CRM charges beyond your Slack plan.
